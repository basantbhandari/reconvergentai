---

## How to Contribute

You can contribute in several ways:

- **Bug Reports & Feature Requests:** Open an [issue](https://github.com/<your-username>/reconvergentai/issues) with a clear description.  
- **Code Contributions:** Improve algorithms, fix bugs, or implement new features.  
- **Documentation:** Enhance clarity, examples, and tutorials.  
- **Experiments & Reproductions:** Share notebooks, scripts, or results that reproduce or extend the original research.

---

## Reporting Issues

When reporting a bug or issue, please include:

- A clear title and description  
- Steps to reproduce the issue  
- Expected vs. actual behavior  
- Environment details (Python version, OS, dependencies, etc.)
